Blog Entry #1 4/5/20
Under Quarantine

This screenshot was captured by Stardew valley developer ConcernedApe. It was found through Google Images on thissite.

It has been two weeks since the last time I went outside. I have been playing Stardew Valley non-stop. When will I get ten hearts with Alex? Can I get that cauliflower for Jodie? How long until I can upgrade my backpack? These are the only things that matter to me now. My life solely consists of farming, mining, foraging and fishing. What does the real world look like? It's been so long that I don't think I can even remember anymore.

Blog Entry #2 4/7/20
Berenstein or Berenstain?

This image of the Berenstain bears was found on the Berenstain Bears wikipedia page. The image was originally uploaded on the Berenstain Bears facebook page.

For eons man has faced many a question. Is humanity inherently evil? Is there a god? Is there a purpose of living? Among all these conundrums has been one greater one to rule them all. Berenstein or Berenstain? My entire life has been haunted by this question. For years, I believed it to be the Berenstein Bears. But this entire time, it's been Berenstain? Impossible. It can not be. What's next? Is Abraham Lincoln actually named Abrahem Lincoln? Ridiculous. There must be a reason that I believed it to be Berenstain. Has the government been brainwashing us this entire time? And if so, for what purpose? To brainwash an entire generation of children? That must be it.
